## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a shift?
Tap the **+** button on the main screen to log a new shift. Enter your start/end time, tips, and any notes, then save.

### How does iCloud sync work?
If you're signed into iCloud and have iCloud Drive enabled, Tip Sheet 2 automatically syncs your data across all of your Apple devices using CloudKit. No setup required.

### Can I track multiple jobs?
Yes. Go to **Jobs** to add as many jobs as you need. Each job can have its own hourly rates, positions, overtime rules, and tip-out rules.

### How do I export my data?
Go to **Settings → Export Data** to generate a CSV of your shifts, which you can open in Numbers, Excel, or any spreadsheet app.

### What does Tip Sheet 2 Pro unlock?
Tip Sheet 2 Pro is a one-time purchase that removes the free tier's 15-shift limit. It's a lifetime unlock — no subscription.

### I purchased Pro but it's not showing up.
Open **Settings → Restore Purchases**. If the issue persists, contact us at support@tipsheetapp.com with your Apple ID email and we'll help sort it out.

### Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If iCloud is enabled, it also syncs to your personal iCloud account. The developer never has access to your data.

### How do I delete my data?
Deleting the app removes all local data. To also remove iCloud-synced data, go to iOS **Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Tip Sheet 2** and delete the data there.
